About Me

Hello! I'm Chen Wangyu, an industrial design student passionate about creating functional and aesthetically pleasing products that solve real-world problems.

My design philosophy centers around user-centric approaches, sustainability, and innovation. I believe good design should be intuitive, accessible, and make a positive impact on people's lives.

With expertise in various design tools and methodologies, I strive to bridge the gap between form and function, creating products that are both beautiful and practical.
